### **One room at a time**

One of the most significant advantages of decluttering is the increased amount of free space in your home. Even better, the house will smell clean and devoid of dust. Viewers will be able to visualize themselves living in the house if it is neat, and they will be able to visualize where their own stuff could go if it is presented in this manner.

Keeping this in mind, begin by decluttering one room at a time. Choose the simplest room to clean first so you can get a head start. Simply place an empty bin in the room and make decluttering the space a top priority for the entire family.

Move on to room #2, #3, and so on once room #1 has been properly decluttered.

### **Choose a Weekend for De-Cluttering**

People these days work really hard, and the last thing many of them want to do on a weeknight is decluttering. Setting aside a weekend to declutter ahead of time is one approach to prevent this problem. You may make significant progress in just a few weekends if you do it this way. Make sure you plan ahead of time for these declutter weekends.

### **Visualize the Clutter-Free Room**

Imagine how the area would look if it were fully clutter-free. This is a really effective strategy for assuring a clutter-free space. What would happen to the furniture? To take use of the natural light, where should all of the necessary items in the space be placed?

Once you've visualized the area as clutter-free in an ideal environment, you can start planning ahead to make this vision a reality.
